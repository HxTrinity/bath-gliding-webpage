Welcome to the University of Bath Gliding Club!

Hey! Welcome, or welcome back, to the University of Bath! Are you wondering what crazy hobby to take up next, are you an aviation fanatic, or do you simply want to have an unusual day out? Look no further - the Gliding Club can cater for all of those!

Have a look through our website and check out our Instagram - @unibathgliding - to find out more about us. If you have any questions, drop us an email at su-gliding@bath.ac.uk.

What We Do

Sustainable Flying 🍃 We fly from Bath Wilts and North Dorset Gliding Club. The club has 3 two-seater and 3 single-seater gliders, a touring motor glider, winch launch, and aerotows.
Taster & Training Flights ✈️ All instruction is given free by volunteers. You can learn to fly gliders for a very reasonable price and work through the syllabus towards flying solo. Or experience a taster flight!
Stunning Views 🌄 Soar over beautiful countryside and have views of the Bristol Channel!
