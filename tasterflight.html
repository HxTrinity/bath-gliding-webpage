Taster Days 🛩

For first-time flyers, we have arranged taster days with our host club. These days are an excellent introduction to gliding and give you a real taste of what the sport is all about!

For your taster flight, you will be towed up to 2,000ft, giving you a wonderful view and plenty of time in the air.

2,000ft aerotow altitude
£50 all-inclusive price
40 Minutes Max flight time, heavily depdenent on weather

Note

Registering your interest does not guarantee a taster flight on the next flying weekend. Availability depends on the weather and the number of people already in the queue. Flight time varies with conditions too. The maximum for a taster flight is 40 minutes, though you may not always reach that. A 2,000 ft aerotow typically gives you plenty of altitude and a great experience regardless!

Register your interest

Prices 💷

You will be charged a fixed price of £50. Taster flights normally cost over £100, so going through the University is significantly cheaper!

Before your taster flight, you will need:

Weight check Between 45 kg / 7 stone and 102 kg / 16 stone
£50 taster ticket Purchased in advance via the SU shop
Confirmation from SU Gliding That you are attending this weekend

Training Programme Pilots!

If you are on our training programme (Newbie to Solo, Solo to SPL, or already licensed), this ticket is not for you. Training programme members should manage their gliding account here instead.
